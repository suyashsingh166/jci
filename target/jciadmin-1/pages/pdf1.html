PNG SAFETY TIPS & CONDITIONS FOR CUSTOMER
{% if data.0.customer_sign %} {% endif %}
1. Don't cover/conceal any part (Meter/Regulator/Pipe/Fitting) of Installation either in balcony or inside the house.
2. PNG should always be used in proper ventilated area, so that PNG can be dispersed in atmosphere. So, please do not cover the ventilation in future.
3. Avoid storing combustible articles/ heating emitting devices close to PNG installation which includes Appliance/ stove, regulator, meter, GI Pipe etc.
4. Do not light any fire or burn garbage, dry vegetation near gas pipeline, meter, riser or regulator.
5. Ensure that Rubber Tube must be of approved quality (Wire braided SURAKSHA Hose) and maximum length should be about 1.5 meters.
6. Keep rubber tube away from heat, water & electrical wire and make sure it does not get heated by the burner, or is looped/ twisted.
7. If you smell gas/ leakage- (a) Do not operate electricalswitches. (b) Ensure that stove knobs are in OFF position. (c) Do not light a matchstick even to detect the leakage of PNG and (d) Open all doors and windows.
8. If the smell persists, immediately close appliance valve & isolation valve installed before meter and call Emergency No-155216 or 1800-111-817 and your area PNG control room as :- (Central Delhi -8130995001, South Delhi-8130995002, West Delhi-8130995003, North Delhi-8130995004, East Delhi- 8130995005, Greater Noida -8130995006, Ghaziabad -8130995007 and Noida-8130995008).
9. Never try to Repair, modify, Adjust or inspect any part of the IGL Installation or allow unauthorized mechanics to do so. Allow authorized IGL technician to inspect the installation by calling our 24X7 customer care number-1800-1025-109.
10. Always keep the appliance valve (connecting gas supply through rubber tube) closed after use or Never leave the appliance value in ON position after cooking is over or during Night.
11. Never leave the cooking appliance (stove) unattended when in use and if the gas supply shut-offsuddenly then close the appliance value, call customer care/Emergency Contact No. of your area.
12. Take permission from IGL before doing any excavation or digging work near IGL Gas Pipeline. Please call 155216 or 1800-111-817 incase of Emergency (For information related to digging activity in the periphery of IGL Pipeline Network or information of gas leakage inside etc.
13. The request for disconnection of gas connection shall be entertained on working days between 10:00 am and 5:00 pm only. The consumer shall inform the date and time of disconnection 48 hours in advance to IGL's Customer Care Centre. The NOC will be issued on working days between 10:00 am and 5:00 pm only and the issue of NOC for CPWD Flats willrequire at least 04 days notice.
